About Us

Lamjung Driving School/Institute established in 2007 is located in Gandaki Province. It is designed on an open area of around 15 ropani located in Lekhnath 27, Talchowk. Here, you will be able to learn driving skills from experienced tutors with 10 years of experience. This school provides driving lessons for Cars, Vans, Jeeps, Scooters, and Bikes.
